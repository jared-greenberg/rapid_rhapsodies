Rapid Rhapsodies

Object of the Game

Clear as many rows as possible before the timer runs out!

Game Play

To clear a row, you must play all music notes within the green box by activating the piano keys directly below them. If multiple notes appear in the same row, you must play them all simultaneously. The timer will begin counting down when you play your first correct note.

Controls

You control the piano by pressing A, S, D, F, or G on your computer's keyboard. The piano keys are labelled with the letter that will activate them. To play multinote combinations, all keyboard keys must be pressed at exactly the same time.

Errors

Pressing incorrect keys or failing to play all notes in the row will result in a penalty delay. During the penalty delay you will not be able to play any notes. You will only be able to resume play when the green box reappears.

Difficulty

The level of difficulty corresponds to the maximum number of notes that could appear in any row: Beginner - 1, Intermediate - 2, Virtuoso - 3.
