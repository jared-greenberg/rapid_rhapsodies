Instructions

  • Objective

    Press as many correct key-combinations before the time runs out. The timer will start when you make the first correct move. Any incorrect combos will prevent you from continuing for about 3 seconds. Notes that you missed will be highlighted in white and ones that were extra will be red.

    Levels

    Each level has a maximum number of keys per move.

    Easy: 1

    Medium: 2

    Hard: 3
